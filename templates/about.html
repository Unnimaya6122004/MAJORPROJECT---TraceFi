Platform Overview

Security intelligence built to stay ahead of DDoS threats

TraceFi brings together detection, forecasting, and reporting so teams can identify incidents earlier and communicate them more clearly.

Mission

Proactive defense, not reactive guesswork

The platform is designed to help organizations interpret live network signals, detect active attacks, and forecast risk patterns before they turn into larger incidents.

Why It Matters

Confidence-backed decision support

Instead of surfacing raw classifications alone, TraceFi emphasizes confidence scores, attack type visibility, and summaries that help analysts prioritize the next step.

Core Features

What the platform delivers

Real-Time Detection

Flag suspicious flows as they happen with a presentation layer optimized for clarity during active monitoring.

Predictive Forecasting
